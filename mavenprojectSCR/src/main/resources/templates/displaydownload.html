Download PowerPoint Document

  • Select the Download option from the SCR to begin.
  • Wait for your browser to automatically place the document in your system's Download folder.
  • Ensure Microsoft PowerPoint is installed on your device to open and view the file.
  • Navigate to your default Downloads folder to locate the completed transfer.
  • Open Sample.pptx using Microsoft PowerPoint to maintain all original formatting and transitions.
